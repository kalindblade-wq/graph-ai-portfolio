The AI Curriculum

Generative AI for Work & Research: No-Code AI Tools for Task Automation

This 8-week guided course focuses on mastering no-code AI tools to automate complex tasks in professional and research settings, emphasizing practical application and the deployment of autonomous AI agents.

Key Focus Areas (Modules)

M1

Content Generation & Documents

Generating structured documents (Markdown, LaTeX), building simple research websites with LLMs, and designing clear presentations/posters.

M2

AI-Automated Data Analysis

Automating data cleaning, running statistical analyses, and visualizing results to amplify research efficiency.

M3

Advanced Prompt Engineering

Techniques for maximizing LLM output quality, control, and reliability for complex scientific tasks.

M4

API Integration Mini-Project

Integrating external services and custom code with LLMs via APIs to create powerful hybrid tools.

M5

AI Automation & Agents

Building AI agents for complex automation flows, workflow orchestration, and browser control for web-based tasks.

M6

GenAI for Communication & Outreach

Creating AI-generated research summaries, scientific figures, and video abstracts for effective research dissemination.

M7-8

Capstone Project & Final Showcase

Collaborating, refining, and presenting a final, complete AI solution or tool built during the course, culminating in a final showcase.
