About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research: No-Code AI Tools for Task Automation

This 8-week guided course focuses on mastering no-code AI tools to automate complex tasks in professional and research settings. It emphasizes practical application, from generating structured documents to deploying autonomous AI agents.

Key Focus Areas (Modules)

Module 1: Content Generation

Generating Markdown, LaTeX, and HTML outputs. Building simple research websites with LLMs, and designing clear presentations/posters.

Module 2: AI-Automated Data Analysis

Automating data cleaning, running statistical analyses, and visualizing results using AI tools.

Module 5: AI Automation & Agents

Introduction to agent-based systems. Building AI agents for complex automation flows and browser control for web-based tasks.

Creating AI-generated research summaries, designing scientific figures, and producing video abstracts for research dissemination.

Module 7: Capstone Project

The final module involves collaborating, refining, and presenting a final, complete AI solution or tool built during the course.
